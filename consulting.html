Services

Complex Patent Matters

Strategic counsel on patent prosecution involving AI, machine learning, neural networks, and emerging technologies. I help firms navigate subject matter eligibility, claim strategy, and portfolio development.

Trademark Strategy

Guidance on trademark registration, enforcement, and dispute resolution. My research on the DuPont factors provides unique insight into likelihood of confusion analysis and TTAB practice.

IP Portfolio Management

Advisory services on building, maintaining, and optimizing intellectual property portfolios aligned with business objectives, from startup foundations to Fortune 50 global strategies.

Attorney Development

Training, mentorship, and work product review for associates. Detailed feedback on patent applications, office action responses, and trademark filings to accelerate professional growth.

International IP Coordination

Support for firms handling multi-jurisdictional patent and trademark matters, coordinating with foreign counsel on global IP protection strategies.

Practice Optimization

Advisory on IP practice structure, workflow efficiency, and technology integration to modernize prosecution while maintaining quality.
